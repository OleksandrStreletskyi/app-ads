CHERNOFEAR: Evil of Pripyat

Welcome to CHERNOFEAR: Evil of Pripyat, an exciting post-apocalyptic zombie shooter set in the dangerous lands of the Chernobyl Exclusion Zone.

You play as Striker, an elite operative whose helicopter crashes into an anomaly. Now the mission changes — survive and uncover the truth.

Main Features:

  • ☢ Intriguing story
  • ☢ Exploration of Pripyat and the Zone
  • ☢ Survival mechanics
  • ☢ Anomalies and radiation
  • ☢ Rich weapon arsenal
  • ☢ First / Third person gameplay
  • ☢ Trading and looting
  • ☢ Dangerous quests
  • ☢ Two endings

Every step could be your last in the Zone...
