Welcome to CHERNOFEAR: Evil of Pripyat

Game Description

Welcome to CHERNOFEAR: Evil of Pripyat, an exciting post-apocalyptic zombie shooter that takes you to the dangerous lands of the Chernobyl Exclusion Zone.

You play as Stryker, an elite operative who is assigned a secret mission in the abandoned zone. However, your journey is cut short when a helicopter crashes into an airborne anomaly. You are the only survivor and must now navigate the unknown to complete your mission.

Main Features of the Game:

  • ☢ Intriguing Story: Fight against zombies, mutants, and bandits while uncovering the secrets of the exclusion zone.
  • ☢ Explore Pripyat and the Zone: Roam abandoned cities like Pripyat, empty villages, military complexes, and secret bunkers full of deadly threats.
  • ☢ Survival in Harsh Conditions: Scavenge for weapons and resources, fight for your life, and stay alive in the hostile environment.
  • ☢ Anomalies and Radiation: The zone is teeming with dangers beyond enemies – deadly anomalies and radiation that threaten your survival.
  • ☢ Rich Arsenal: Access a wide range of weapons from pistols to powerful gauss rifles. Customize and upgrade them for greater firepower.
  • ☢ First or Third Person View: Choose between a first-person view for immersive gameplay or a third-person view for enhanced control.
  • ☢ Trading and Resource Hunting: Explore hidden geocaches, trade with merchants, and acquire valuable items to survive.
  • ☢ Exciting Quests: Complete dangerous missions in the most remote areas of the zone, facing many challenges along the way.
  • ☢ Two Endings: Your decisions lead to one of two endings – save the zone or plunge it into chaos forever.

Prepare for a dangerous journey through the exclusion zone, where every step could be your last. Will you uncover the secrets of Pripyat and survive in this brutal world?
